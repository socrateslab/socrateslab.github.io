About

The Computational Communication Collaboratory (CCC, C-Cube, or C3) was established in February 2015 by the School of Journalism and Communication, Nanjing University.

Vision

  • To uncover the computational gene of human communication behaviors.

workshops

  • Introduction to Computational Communication
  • Data Journalism
  • Computational Advertising
  • Big Data Analysis

Research Interests

  • Computational Communication Research
  • Computational Narrative
  • Computational Methods
  • Network Science
  • Digital Media and Social Time
